I am Russell Strand, oil painter and art educator. I live in Tillamook County, Oregon and enjoy capturing the light and shadow of Pacific Northwest industrial landscapes. I love teaching oil painting to the community in a way that allows individuals to explore art in their own way and discover and express their unique creative voice.
