I was lucky to be tasked with the hands-on construction of an agricultural laboratory interior in 2017-18. I was responsible for leading the assembly of wall panels, fabricating all window boxes and related trim to seal openings, installing metal sinks and all cabinetry, glazing all borrowed lites, interior door installation with related hardware including closers, installing specialty sliding door units, Designing and installing a sliding door seal system for a refrigerated room, and installing extensive exterior door seals.
